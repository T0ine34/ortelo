Ortelo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 24th February 2024

Thank you for using Ortelo, an online gaming application. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect:

1. Google Account Information:
- When you choose to log in with Google, we collect your name to use as your username and your email to associate with your Ortelo account.

How We Use Your Information:

1. User Identification:
- We use your Google name as the username within the Ortelo app.
- Your email is used as the primary contact for your Ortelo account.

2. Service Improvement:
- We may analyze user data in an aggregated, anonymized form to improve and enhance the Ortelo gaming experience.

Data Security:

1. Secure Storage:
- Your information is securely stored on our servers and is only accessible to authorized personnel.

2. Third-Party Services:
- We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

Your Choices:

1. Account Deletion:
- You can request the deletion of your Ortelo account at any time. Please contact us at sae.ortello@gmail.com for assistance.

Updates to this Privacy Policy:

1. Notification:
- Users will be notified of any changes to this Privacy Policy via email or within the Ortelo app.

2. Review:
- It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sae.ortello@gmail.com.

Thank you for choosing Ortelo!
